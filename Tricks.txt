http://www.calvertnet.k12.md.us/[a-z]+/[a-z]+/[a-z]+/[a-z]+/[a-z]+/([a-z]+).pdf

This looks for a file with numbers or letters in the name
http://www.calvertnet.k12.md.us/[a-z]+/[a-z]+/[a-z]+/([a-zA-Z0-9]+).jpg

Getting half the right half of a table when it has a break in the second cell of each row:
F: (?s)<tr>.+?<td>.+?<td><br>.+?</td>
R:

first letter until link: 
F: (s?)[0-9a-zA-Z]+.+? h
R: (s?).+? http

Replacing something that is the text of an <a> tag with <p> tag
F: (s?)<p>(.+?) <a href="(.+?)">(.+?)</a></p>
R: <p>\2<a href="\3">\2</a></p>
